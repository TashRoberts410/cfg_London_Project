Accommodation

For those on a budget, or looking to meet new friends, hostels are a great option.
YHA - These hostels are great for both individuals and families. Good kitchen facilities means you can save a bit of money by preparing your own meals.
Generator - London - A great hostel for those that want to socialise and meet people.

Chain hotels offer clean and comfortable rooms
Travelodge and Premier Inn - Both offer well-equipped family rooms in locations all across London, and offer breakfasts.

Hotels for those who want five star service and for whom budget is no issue
Claridges - Grand English style, timeless glamour and impeccable service.
Shangri-la - Located inside the Shard this five-star modern hotel has stunning views of London.
